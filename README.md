> A content platform for architects, interior designers, and spatial designers

#### News
- Curated articles and news related to architecture, interior design, and spatial trends

#### Competitions
- Information on architecture, space, and design competitions

#### Resources
- Curated CAD and Illustrator resources for design work

#### Tips
- Modeling and rendering tip videos for architecture and interior design

#### Online Courses
- Modeling and rendering courses tailored for architects and spatial designers

#### Community Board / Online Reviews
- Free resume and portfolio feedback for designers / students related to spatial major
